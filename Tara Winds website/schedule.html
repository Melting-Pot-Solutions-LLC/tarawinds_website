About Tara Winds

Tara Winds is an all-adult symphonic band celebrating over 25 years of excellence in musical performance. Our founders, Larry Volman, Ed Davis, and David Gregory envisioned Tara Winds to be a performance medium for local musicians and to encourage school band members to continue performing after completion of their formal studies.
