2017

Tara Winds Veteran's Day Concert

November 12, 2017 @ 4 PM:
Griffin Auditorium - 234 E Taylor St, Griffin, GA 30224.
You can purchase tickets here; $20 for General Admission

Program
  • The Star Spangled Banner arr. by John Williams
  • Rocky Point Holiday by Ron Nelson
  • Duty Honor Country text by Douglas MacArthur music by Harold Walters
  • Sons of Veterans March by Karl L. King arr. by Gene Milford
  • Liberty Fanfare by John Williams
  • Glenn Miller in Concert arr. by Paul Murtha
  • Armed Forces Salute arr. by Bob Lowden
  • America Forever March by James V. Colonna
  • Missing Man by Jerker Johansson
  • God Bless America by Irving Berlin arr. by Erik Leidzen
  • The Stars and Stripes Forever by John P. Sousa arr. by John R. Bourgeois
  • America the Beautiful by Samuel A. Ward arr. by Carmen Dragon

* If you are absent more tha 3 rehearsals (25%), you may jeopardize your membership and elibigility to participate in Tara Winds. Dues are non-refundable
