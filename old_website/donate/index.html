♫ Donate

The Tara Winds Scholarship Foundation is designed to share the love of music by providing funds to further music education for middle and high school band students. To that end, the Foundation was formed in 1993 to support this effort. Since its inception, over $100,000 has been awarded in scholarships for middle and high school band students to attend summer music camps and colleges.
